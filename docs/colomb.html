Modified

February 10, 2024

Basic Info.

  • Name: Imperial Federation, Map of the world showing the extent of the British Empire in 1886

  • Who

    • Information Compilation: 존 콜롬 John Colomb (1 May 1838 – 27 May 1909)

    • Drawing: 월터 크레인 Walter Crane (15 August 1845 – 14 March 1915)

  • When: 1886

  • Where: Boston Public Library

    • Graphic: An Illustrated Weekly Newspaper as a color supplement to mark Queen Victoria’s Golden Jubilee (Harwood 2012, 130)

  • Size: 63 x 86 cm
