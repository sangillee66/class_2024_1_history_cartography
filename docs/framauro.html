Basic Info.

  • Name: Fra Mauro map

  • Who: 프라 마우로 Fra Mauro (1400 - 1464)

  • When: 1450년경

    • 1804, Reproduced by British cartographer William Frazer

  • Where: Museo Correr, Venice, Italy

    • Displayed at Biblioteca Marciana, Venice, Italy

    • Reproduced one at the British Library

  • Size: 2.4 x 2.4 m (Brotton 2014, 73)
